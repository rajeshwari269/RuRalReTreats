1. Choose Eco-Friendly Transportation

Use trains, buses, or cycle instead of flights to lower emissions.

2. Pack Light

Lighter bags save fuel. Bring only what you need and reuse items.

3. Stay in Eco-Friendly Lodging

Look for green-certified places using renewable energy.

4. Eat Local & Sustainable

Support local farms, go plant-based, and avoid overfished seafood.

5. Reduce Plastic Waste

Bring reusable bottles, bags, and utensils to cut down on waste.

6. Respect Wildlife & Culture

Stick to trails and avoid animal tourism. Learn cultural norms.

7. Offset Your Emissions

Donate to projects that plant trees or use clean energy to balance your trip.

8. Support Local Businesses

Buy souvenirs from local artists and use local guides.

9. Save Energy & Water

Turn off lights, reuse towels, and conserve water in hotels.

10. Share & Inspire

Share your green travels to inspire others to go eco-friendly!
