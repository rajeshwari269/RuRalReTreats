1. Mawlynnong, Meghalaya

Known as "Asia‚Äôs Cleanest Village", Mawlynnong offers scenic beauty, Living Root Bridges, and panoramic views of Bangladesh.

Things to Do:

  • Visit the Living Root Bridges
  • Enjoy panoramic views from the Sky Walk
  • Explore local Khasi culture and food

2. Khimsar, Rajasthan

Experience the golden dunes, camel safaris, and Rajasthani heritage in this desert oasis.

Things to Do:

  • Stay in the heritage Khimsar Fort
  • Enjoy a sunset camel safari
  • Visit the sand dunes of Khimsar

3. Ziro Valley, Arunachal Pradesh

A UNESCO nominee with rolling green hills, pine forests, and the famous Ziro Music Festival.

Things to Do:

  • Attend the Ziro Music Festival
  • Visit the Apatani tribal villages
  • Hike through the lush forests of Ziro

4. Kutch Villages, Gujarat

Home to the vast white salt desert and rich traditional handicrafts.

Things to Do:

  • Witness the Rann Utsav festival
  • Explore the handicraft villages of Kutch
  • Visit the Great Rann of Kutch

5. Spiti Valley, Himachal Pradesh

A high-altitude Himalayan wonderland with ancient monasteries and breathtaking landscapes.

Things to Do:

  • Visit the Key Monastery
  • Explore the Tabo Monastery
  • Camp by the stunning Chandratal Lake
